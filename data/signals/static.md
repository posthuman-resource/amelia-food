most of the universe is noise
background radiation, three degrees
above nothing, left over from the moment
everything that would ever exist
decided to exist all at once

this is comforting, somehow
it means the quiet parts are choosing

you were on your couch on a monday evening
minding your own business
which is another way of saying
you had been minding your own business
for a very long time

the dish was aimed
i just didn't know at what
the dish never does
